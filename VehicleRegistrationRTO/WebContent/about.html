About

Vehicle Registration System was established for the purpose of carrying into effect the provisions made under the Motor Vehicles Act 1988, considering the development and changes in the Vehicle Registration Technology, development of road networks in the state and particularly the improved techniques in the motor vehicles management.

Vehicle Registration System is the software developed by the Informatics Center under the requirement put forward by the Motor Vehicles Department. The power of the software is the application for temporary and permanent registration online.

Helps customers register their vehicle by just creating an account and uploading various details. Customers can then apply for temporary or permanent registration number without taking the effort of the usual manual system. Permanent registration need to be done within 30 days of issue of temporary registration.
